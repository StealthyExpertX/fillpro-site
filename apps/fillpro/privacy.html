FillPro Privacy Policy

FillPro keeps profile data local to the extension on your device. This page covers what is stored, what is skipped, what touches third parties, and how local data is managed.

Effective: April 26, 2026 · Last updated: April 26, 2026

Privacy snapshot

What matters most.

  • Profiles and uploads stay local to the extension on your device.
  • FillPro does not collect form values in the background.
  • Support drafts only open when you choose to open them.

What FillPro stores

Only what the product needs to fill forms.

  • Profile data: names, email addresses, phone numbers, and other fields you enter into profiles.
  • Captured form data: values saved when you choose Save current form.
  • Attached uploads: local file copies used for matching upload inputs you choose to fill.
  • Smart rules: custom match patterns and values for awkward field names.
  • Usage counters: free-tier profile count state stored locally.

What FillPro skips

Sensitive fields are intentionally off-limits.

FillPro is designed not to autofill or save passwords, payment card numbers, security codes, Social Security numbers, tax IDs, one-time codes, and similar credential or payment inputs.

Data sharing

No background collection or sale of profile data.

FillPro does not collect, sell, or share your saved profile data, captured form values, or attached files with third parties. Limited network activity only happens for optional Pro licensing with ExtensionPay or when you intentionally open a support email draft.

Optional Pro billing

Third-party payment handling stays separate.

If you upgrade to Pro, payment is handled by ExtensionPay and Stripe. FillPro may store license status and related restore metadata locally so paid access can be recognized, but it does not store full payment details.

Retention and deletion

Free-use data is managed on your device.

Your saved profiles, rules, and attached uploads remain on your device until you remove them. Because that data stays local, support cannot remove it remotely. Delete profiles inside FillPro or uninstall the extension to remove extension-managed storage. Exported files you save outside the extension remain wherever you put them.

Diagnostics

Only when you opt in.

If you use the feedback flow, FillPro can prepare an email draft with optional diagnostics you explicitly choose to include. Those may contain page title, site path, visible field metadata, framework hints, browser details, and extension version. Field values are not included.

Permissions

Permission scope stays narrow.

Permission Why FillPro uses it
activeTab Required to fill form fields on the current page when you click Fill Page.
scripting Required to run the form-filling logic on the current page.
contextMenus Required to add the Save current form as profile right-click option.
storage Required to save profiles, rules, uploads, and usage state locally.
alarms Required to schedule the optional 3-day re-engagement reminder. No external network use.

Contact

Need a privacy answer?

Use the support page contact form and choose the Privacy topic. That opens a draft to the right inbox without publishing raw mailbox strings in the public page text.
