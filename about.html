About

Hi, I'm Karl.

I'm a solo indie dev. I build small browser extensions that do one thing well and don't watch you while they're at it.

In short

One person, small tools, no tracking.

  • One developer. No team.
  • No analytics. No telemetry. No email gate.
  • Every product stores its data on your device.

Why I'm doing this

Most extensions ask for too much.

I kept seeing simple browser tools that wanted full page access, account sign-ups, and analytics SDKs just to do something basic. I wanted to ship the opposite — small extensions that do one job, ask for the minimum, and never phone home.

So Stealthy Apps is the home for that. FillPro is the first one. More are coming.

How I work

Plain rules.

Local-only by default

Your data lives in your browser. I don't run servers that store user data.

No analytics

No Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, no event tracking. The only third party in the loop is Stripe (via ExtensionPay) for optional Pro upgrades.

Real free tier

Free is meant to be usable, not a teaser. Pro adds breathing room for power users.

I read every email

Use any product's contact form. It comes to me.
